NHG YOUTH LAB 2026

Welcome to NHG Youth Lab 2026 Hackathon, where innovation meets public health. This year is our first time holding this hackathon, where we are bringing together youths aged 18 to 28 across the nation to tackle the modern youth health crisis.

In partnership with the National Healthcare Group (NHG), this hackathon provides the tools, mentorship, and training needed to build the next generation of youth changemakers. This hackathon involves deep youth interaction with their local communities to identify pressing needs and engineer meaningful solutions.

Participants are required to craft a problem statement out of the theme of youth health crisis. Through their identified problem statement, participants identify root causes and develop solutions to address such root causes. Scores will be given out based on three criterias: crafting of problem statement and how connected root causes are to the problem statement; quality and innovativeness of the solution; and feasibility of solution. You can find further details in the bottom of the page.

Event Rundown

Day 1: June 15 | Ideation

Kickoff & Problem Scoping

Registration starts at 0830hrs. Join clinical mentors from NHG to understand pressing youth health gaps. Formation of teams and deep-dive into deep brainstorming sessions and come up with a problem statement and solution idea.

Day 2: June 16 | Prototyping

Build & Validate

Intensive rapid prototyping. Access to UI/UX workshops and direct feedback sessions with healthcare mentors to refine your solution's feasibility and appearance.

Day 3: June 17 | Final Pitch

Presentation

Final polishing of prototypes. Teams will pitch to a panel of judges from NYC, NHG, and industry professionals. Award ceremony and networking dinner to follow.
