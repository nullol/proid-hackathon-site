About

Welcome to NHG Youth Lab, where innovation meets public health. We are a hackathon, bringing together youths aged 18 to 28 across the nation to tackle the modern youth health crisis.

In partnership with the National Healthcare Group (NHG), this hackathon provides the tools, mentorship, and training needed to build the next generation of youth changemakers. This hackathon involves deep youth interaction with their local communities to identify pressing needs and engineer meaningful solutions.

Our Goal

Founded in 2026, NHG Youth Lab began as a grassroots initiative born out of a critical observation during the post-pandemic transition. Our journey has been defined by a commitment to move beyond awareness toward action—bridging student creativity with clinical expertise to forge the next generation of healthcare leaders.
