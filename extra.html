Frequently Asked Questions

What is the schedule of the event?

The hackathon runs from June 15 to June 17, 2026. Registration opens daily at 0830hrs at the NHG Headquarters. Day 1 is focused on ideation, Day 2 on prototyping, and Day 3 concludes with the final pitching and awards ceremony.

When will I receive my certificate?
Digital Certificates of Participation will be emailed to all attendees within 14 to 21 working days after the event. Physical certificates for winners will be presented during the Award Ceremony on Day 3.
How will judging be conducted?
Judging is based on a structured rubric: 40% Clinical Impact, 30% Innovation, 20% Feasibility, and 10% Presentation. Our panel consists of representatives from NHG, NYC, and industry leaders.
